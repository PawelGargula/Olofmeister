Olof Kajbjer Gustafsson

Wiki

"Olof "olofmeister" Kajbjer Gustafsson (formerly known as olofm) (born January 31, 1992) is a Swedish professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and former professional Counter-Strike player who plays for FaZe Clan as a supportive rifler." (liquipedia)

"That comes after his legendary 3-year-long tenure with Fnatic, during which he won two Majors — ESL One: Katowice 2015 and ESL One: Cologne 2015 — and numerous other international tournaments, and was named the best player in the world of 2015." (liquipedia)
